OLD BUT GOLD

In each fold of time, OLD BUT GOLD seamlessly intertwines the imprints of the past into the rhythm of the present. This collection is a profound tribute to the timeless beauty of bygone eras, while asserting trends in every contemporary line.

This time, Mikenco ventures into previously unexplored silhouettes such as boxy, flares, baggy… accompanied by the refined choice of canvas material, evoking a sense of nostalgia. OLD BUT GOLD is not only a blend of the old and the new but also a collection that honors enduring values over time.
